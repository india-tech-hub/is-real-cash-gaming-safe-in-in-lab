## Legal Basis

### Supreme Court Rulings (Skill vs. Chance)
- **State of Andhra Pradesh v. K. Satyanarayana (1968)**: Established that Rummy is a game of skill, not chance.
- **2015 Re-affirmation**: SC reconfirmed skill-based games (Rummy, Teen Patti, Poker) are legal when played for stakes in states without specific prohibitions.

## Game Classifications

### Skill-Based Games (Generally Legal)
- Rummy (all variants)
- Teen Patti
- Poker
- Bridge

### Games of Pure Chance (Restricted in Most States)
- Colour Prediction apps
- Aviator (crash games)
- Slot machines
- Keno

### Lottery (State-Specific)
- Legal only where state lottery commission operates
- Online lottery platforms face variable enforcement
